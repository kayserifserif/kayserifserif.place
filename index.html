Hi :) I’m a creative technologist interested in designing and developing for interactive or experiential media.

Born and raised in Hong Kong with roots in Shanghai, I’m currently a fourth-year Media Arts + Practice student in the USC School of Cinematic Arts, minoring in computer programming and linguistics.

I love making imaginative and thoughtful media that engages people, tells stories, and explores ideas in interesting ways.

I’m also passionate about accessibility, urbanism, environmentalism, and internet culture.

In my personal creative practice, I’m particularly interested in poetic tools and the ethos of “move slow; fix things”. Current research interests include: critical code studies, internet culture and language, interface aesthetics, electronic literature, and poetic tech.
